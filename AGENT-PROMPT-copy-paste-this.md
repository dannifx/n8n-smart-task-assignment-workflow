## Copy Everything Below This Line

I want to set up the Smart Task Assignment Workflow for my team.

CONTEXT:
I have access to the complete workflow documentation in this folder, including:
- ai-guided-setup-interview.md (interview questions and configuration guide)

5. After completing all sections, generate a "Configuration Summary" document
6. Create customized n8n workflow guides adapted to my specific setup

IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS:
- Only ask questions from ONE section at a time, then wait for my responses
- If my system differs significantly from TeamworkFX, help me map the equivalent concepts

Let's start with Section 1: Project Management System.
Ask me the questions from that section.

## What Happens Next?

Your AI assistant will:

1. ✅ Read the interview guide
2. 💬 Ask you questions about your team, tools, and preferences
3. 📝 Document your configuration choices
4. 🔧 Generate customized workflow guides for your setup
5. 👨‍🏫 Walk you through building each workflow in n8n
6. 🧪 Help you test and troubleshoot
7. 📊 Provide maintenance guidance for the future

## Before You Start

**Have this information ready (if available):**

- [ ] Your project management system name (Asana, ClickUp, Jira, etc.)
- [ ] API documentation or MCP server details for your PM system
- [ ] List of team members who will be included in the assignment system
- [ ] API key for your AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- [ ] Slack workspace access (if using Slack) OR alternative notification platform
- [ ] n8n account (Cloud or self-hosted)

**Don't worry if you don't have everything yet** — the AI will guide you to get what's needed.

---

## Interview Duration

Expect **30-45 minutes** for the full interview, broken into manageable sections.

You can pause and resume anytime. Just tell the AI "Let's take a break" and when you return, say "Let's continue where we left off."

---

## After the Interview

You'll receive:

1. **Configuration Summary Document** — All your answers documented in one place
2. **Customized Workflow Guides** — Step-by-step n8n build instructions adapted for your team
3. **Field Mapping Reference** — How your PM system's fields map to the workflow
4. **Testing Checklist** — What to verify before going live

Then the AI will guide you through building each workflow, node by node.

---

## Tips for a Smooth Setup

✅ **Be specific about your team's workflow** — The more detail you provide, the better the AI can customize

✅ **Ask questions if something is unclear** — The AI can explain concepts or suggest alternatives

✅ **Have your PM system open** — You may need to check field names, IDs, or settings during the interview

✅ **Test as you build** — Follow the AI's guidance to test each workflow after building it

✅ **Document what you learn** — If something differs from expectations, the AI will update guides in real-time
